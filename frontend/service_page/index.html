How It Works

TWO LINES OF DEFENSE.
ZERO BLIND SPOTS.

The supervised branch—an aggressively tuned XGBoost classifier—learns every historical fraud pattern in Vietnam. It operates strictly on certainty, catching known attacks precisely without generating alert fatigue.

The unsupervised branch—a Deep Autoencoder—monitors behavioral reconstruction. It flags transactions that simply do not look mathematically normal, catching zero-day attacks your baseline rule-sets have never encountered.

Every flagged incident across both branches automatically maps back to its root cause, instantly generating a clear-text explanation of exactly why the transaction was blocked.

Every flagged transaction includes a SHAP explanation breakdown — satisfying SBV regulatory explainability requirements without additional tooling.
